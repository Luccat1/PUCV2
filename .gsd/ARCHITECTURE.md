## Data Flow

1. **Applicant submits** Google Form → data lands in `Respuestas de formulario 1`
2. **Form trigger** fires `evaluarPostulacionesPUCV2()` → reads new rows, calculates scores
3. **Results written** to `Evaluación automatizada` with scores breakdown
4. **Dashboard regenerated** with statistics, charts, breakdowns
5. **Top 25 written** to `Seleccionados` with acceptance dropdowns
6. **Admin reviews** via Web App → assigns levels, verifies certificates
7. **Emails sent** in batches (selected, waitlist, not-selected) via templates
8. **Waitlist auto-promotes** when selected applicants decline (onEdit trigger)
9. **Final list generated** for accepted participants, organized by level
